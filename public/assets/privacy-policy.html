Privacy Policy for LetsGo

LetsGo is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal information when you use our social network.

What Information Do We Collect?

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account on our social network, we may collect your name, email address, and password.
  • Profile Information: We may collect information that you provide in your profile, such as your photo, location, and other demographic information.
  • User Content: We may collect information that you post on our social network, such as photos, videos, and comments.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our social network, such as the pages you visit, the features you use, and the actions you take.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our social network, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
